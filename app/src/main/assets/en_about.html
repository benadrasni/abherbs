Purpose

Have you ever wondered what is the name of a beautiful flower you have just seen in the park? Application What's that flower? will help you to find it out. Identify flower's color, habitat and number of petals and your options are narrowed to couple of dozens. Then scroll the list and choose right one. Read about it and enjoy power of knowledge. Be the wise one.
